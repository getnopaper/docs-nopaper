# Getting Started

Welcome to NoPaper! This guide will help you start earning B3TR tokens for your sustainable actions.

## Prerequisites

- **VeWorld Wallet**: Download from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.vechain.veworld.app&/) or [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/veworld/id6471963974)
- **Smartphone**: NoPaper is a mobile-first application.

## Connect Your Wallet

1. Open [nopaper.life](https://nopaper.life) on your mobile browser
2. Tap the **Connect Wallet** button
3. Select **VeWorld** from the options
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

::: danger Zero-Tolerance Device & Network Policy
To ensure fair distribution of rewards, we enforce a strict **1 Smartphone + 1 IP + 1 Wallet** policy.
- **Do not use multiple wallets** or multiple devices. Any attempt to bypass limits will result in a permanent ban.
- **Do not use VPNs or Proxies**.
- Switching between Mobile Data and WiFi frequently may trigger security flags. **Stick to one stable connection.**
:::

## Make Your First Submission

### Step 1: Take a Screenshot

Take a **clean digital screenshot** of your receipt. The receipt should be from:

- Organic grocery stores
- Farmers markets
- Sustainable product purchases

::: warning Requirements
- Must be a **digital screenshot** (not a photo of paper)
- No visible phone UI (battery, clock, signal bars)
- No background visible (no table, fingers, wood)
:::

### Step 2: Upload Your Receipt

1. Tap the **Upload Receipt** dropzone
2. Select your screenshot
3. Wait for AI verification (usually 5-10 seconds)

### Step 3: Receive Your Reward

If approved, you'll receive:
- Confirmation with reward amount
- B3TR tokens in your wallet
- Submission added to your history
